Travel & Location

Location

The workshop will be held at the La Posada Resort, Santa Fe, New Mexico. Nestled on six beautifully landscaped acres in the heart of Santa Fe, this AAA Four Diamond name in Santa Fe hotel resorts is a haven of Adobe-style architecture, colorful gardens, and cozy outdoor fireplaces. Here are some features and amenities of the La Posada Resort-

  • World-class RockResorts Spa ®
  • State-of-the-art fitness center
  • Heated outdoor swimming pool and whirlpool
  • Local activities that include golf, art galleries, hiking, skiing, shopping, dining, whitewater rafting, hot-air ballooning, and cultural excursions
  • 4,500-square-foot conference center, ideal for meetings and wedding receptions
  • Two blocks from Santa Fe’s historic Plaza and Canyon Road
  • A short walk from 200 renowned galleries and hundreds of museums, shops, boutiques, and restaurants
  • 20 minutes from Santa Fe Airport and one hour from Albuquerque International Airport (ABQ)
  • 90 minutes south of Taos
  • One of Travel + Leisure’s “500 Greatest Hotels in the World” (2004)

For more information about the hotel, please visit http://laposada.rockresorts.com. On information about how to get there, see the map and directions below.
