US-Spain Bilateral Scientific Workshop on Information Science and Related Technologies



Organizers

  • Prince of Asturias Endowed Chair on Information Science and Related Technologies at the University of New Mexico, currently held by Prof. Fernando Perez-Gonzalez (www.ece.unm.edu/PoA/).
  • The Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of New Mexico (http://www.ece.unm.edu).

Sponsors

  • The Spanish Ministry of Science and Innovation (MICINN)
  • The University of New Mexico
  • The New Mexico Consortium
  • Santander Universities

Event Information

  • Date: December 3-4, 2010
  • Location: La Posada Resort, Santa Fe, New Mexico (http://laposada.rockresorts.com/).


Additional Information

The aim of the workshop is to build bilateral research bridges that will benefit science and technology between the US and Spain, and to generate multidisciplinary projects that combine some or all of the workshop areas, which are:

  • Information and communication theory,
  • High-performance computing and cloud computing,
  • Quantum information processing,
  • Smart grids and ICTs in energy,
  • Bio-inspired networks, internet of things, and the semantic web.

There will be 10 invited presentations by Spanish researchers and 10 by US researchers, each 30 minutes long, leaving plenty of time for personal interaction among the participants.
